Oatmeal

Basic oatmeal with cottage cheese and blueberries for additional protein and micronutrients

Ingredients:

  • 60g oats
  • 3dl water
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 100g cottage cheese
  • handful blueberries

Steps:

  1. add water and oats to a bowl and microwave for 1 minute
  2. add honey to the bowl, mix and put it back in the microwave for 1-2 minutes
  3. add cottage cheese and blueberries
  4. Enjoy your gains!
