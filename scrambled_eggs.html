Scrambled eggs

Simple and easy scrambled eggs with bell peppers.

Ingredients:

  • 3 whole eggs
  • 2 tsp sriracha
  • 1/2 bell pepper

Note: to add protein, add 100-200g egg whites

Steps:

  1. Cut the bell pepper into small pieces
  2. Add eggs and sriracha to a bowl and whisk
  3. Throw the eggs to a pan on medium heat and stir the eggs until almost cooked
  4. add bell peppers to the pan and turn off the heat
  5. Keep stirring the eggs for another 30 sec until ready to serve
